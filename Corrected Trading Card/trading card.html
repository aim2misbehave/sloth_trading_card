Three-Toed Sloth

While sloths are arboreal mammals, they are surprisingly agile swimmers!

  • Scientific Name: Bradypus
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Sleep Pattern: 15-18 hours
  • Life Cycle: 25-30 years

Sloths eat buds, leaves, and shoots, but some sloths have been known to eat insects and birds in case they need more nutrients. Sloths have a very slow metabolism, and sometimes it can take 30 days to digest their food!
